Arizona Family Connections is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all visitors, including individuals with disabilities. We believe everyone deserves equal access to information about family services in our community.

## Our Commitment

We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines help make web content more accessible to people with a wide range of disabilities, including visual, auditory, physical, speech, cognitive, language, learning, and neurological disabilities.

## Accessibility Features

This website includes the following accessibility features:

- Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
- Keyboard navigation support throughout the site
- Sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
- Descriptive alternative text for images
- Clear and consistent navigation
- Resizable text without loss of functionality
- Focus indicators for interactive elements
- Descriptive link text

## Ongoing Improvements

We use Silktide to regularly audit our website's accessibility and identify areas for improvement. Accessibility is an ongoing effort, and we continuously work to enhance the usability of our site for all visitors.

## Need Help Accessing Content?

If you experience difficulty accessing any content on this website, please contact us. We are happy to assist you and provide information in an alternative format.

- **Phone:** (480) 764-6689
- **Email:** contact@arizonafamilyconnections.com

When contacting us, please include:

- The web address (URL) of the content you were trying to access
- A description of the issue you experienced
- Your preferred method of contact

We will respond to your request within 2 business days.

## Third-Party Content

While we strive to ensure accessibility across our entire website, some third-party content or applications may not be fully accessible. We work with our vendors to improve accessibility where possible.

## Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve. If you have suggestions for how we can enhance accessibility on our website, please reach out using the contact information above.
